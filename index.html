About


I am Diana of Themyscira, daughter of Hippolyta, Queen of the Amazons.

I used to want to save the world. To end war and bring peace to mankind.
But then, I glimpsed the darkness that lives within their light. I learned that inside every one of them, there will always be both. The choice each must make for themselves. Something no hero will ever defeat. And now I know.. that only love can truly save the world.
So I stay. I fight. I give.
For the world I know can be. This is my mission now.
Forever
